Application

Please choose the event and type of package you would like to apply for and fill out the forms below to apply.

Packages:

Audition for Active participant:

As there is only a limited number of students, we can accommodate there is a screening round before one can apply for the Active participant package. The option for the Active participant package will only be to those who have passed the audition.

Active participant:

During the 5 days 2*60-minute individual masterclass with the professor.

Optional plus one class:

Option to have one more individual masterclass with the professor during the 5 days.

Piano accompaniment:

Piano accompaniment option for one 60-minute lesson. There is an option to choose for only one 60-minute lesson or for both lessons. For both lessons, please choose piano accompaniment twice.

Passive participants standard:

May visit all the classes during the 5 days as a passive participant.

Passive participants U25:

May visit all the classes during the 5 days as a passive participant for ages 25 and younger.

Optional practice:

60-minute practice in one of the classrooms at the venue, this can be multiple times during the 5 days.

Consultation on studying in Hungary:

45-minute consultation on the basics of studying in Hungary and on applying to the Liszt Ferenc Academy of Music

Daily ticket at the venues:

May visit all the classes during the day as a passive participant. Same day purchase, can be paid only at the venue in cash.

Daily ticket at the venues U25:

May visit all the classes during the day as a passive participant for ages 25 and younger. Same day purchase can be paid only at the venue in cash.

Participant selection will be through video auditions.

Audition requirements for clarinet:

Two freely chosen compositions/movements of different style and character (maximum duration of 20 minutes in total). Piano accompaniment is not obligatory.

Audition requirements for flute:

Two freely chosen compositions/movements of different style and character (maximum duration of 20 minutes in total). Piano accompaniment is not obligatory.

Audition requirements for violin:

Two freely chosen compositions/movements of different style and character (maximum duration of 20 minutes in total). Piano accompaniment is not obligatory.

Audition requirements for piano:

Freely chosen piano piece or pieces with a length of 10-15 minutes
