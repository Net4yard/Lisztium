PRIVACY POLICY

FOR THE MASTER CLASSES ORGANIZED IN THE FRAMEWORK OF “Lisztium in Kumamoto 2025”

This privacy policy complies with both the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI).

The Oresztész Bt. is organizing the "Lisztium in Kumamoto 2025" event in 2025. During this international event great artists will assemble from the classical performance music scene (Gábor Farkas, Vera Oross, Haruka Nagao, Csaba Pálfi). Masterclasses and concerts are parts of this 5-day-long festive programme.

This privacy policy contains information on the data processing of the "Lisztium in Kumamoto 2025" event (hereinafter referred to as the Master Class) in order to provide applicants with the widest array of information possible on the processing of their personal data.
