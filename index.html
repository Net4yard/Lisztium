Welcome to the home of Lisztium masterclasses!

These masterclasses are for those who want to deepen their knowledge of music, and learned from established, well-known musicians. Lisztium is a community where a passion for music and a thirst for learning come together to unite Japanese and Hungarian cultures through the power of music.

Discover the beauty of keyboard and string instruments, where melody and harmony come together. From grand pianos to violins, each instrument offers a unique sound and character.

Explore the rich tones of woodwind and brass wind instruments, where breath and technique create captivating melodies. From elegant flutes, clarinets to trumpets, each instrument brings its own distinct voice to the ensemble. Woodwind and brass wind instruments bring a rich and dynamic sound to orchestras, bands, and solo performances.

Keyboard and String instruments

Keyboard and string instruments have shaped the history of music, bringing emotion and depth to countless compositions. From the delicate tones of a violin to the powerful chords of a grand piano, these instruments offer endless possibilities for musicians. Whether you prefer classical elegance or modern versatility, keyboard and string instruments provide the perfect foundation for musical creativity.

Woodwind and Brasswind instruments

From the sooth melodies of the flute to the powerful sound of the trumpet, woodwind and brass instruments add character and expression to any musical composition. Whether you're working in classical or contemporary music, these instruments offer endless variety and depth. Their distinct timbres and incredible soundscapes make them essential for creating compelling, emotional musical experiences.

What We Offer

  • Individual attention: We believe that every student is unique, which is why our lessons are tailored to your pace. Whether you're passionate about classical music or drawn to modern rhythms, you'll find your place here. Lisztium Music School is not just a school—it's a community where a shared love of music connects us all.
  • Friendly environment: A place where making mistakes is part of the joy, because for us, learning is not stressful—it's an inspiring and motivating journey. Our teachers are dedicated masters of music who share not only their knowledge but also their hearts and souls. With their guidance, you'll explore the hidden paths of music, set your creativity free, and unlock your full potential.
  • Professional support: In addition to our passion for music, we offer years of professional experience to help you develop both your technique and artistic expression. Visit us and discover your inner musical potential! Let the sounds guide you, and let music become the meaning of your life.
